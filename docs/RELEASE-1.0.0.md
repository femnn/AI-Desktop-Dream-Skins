# v1.0.0 · 三平台基础主题 / Three-platform base themes

## 中文

首个公开版本包含：

- Codex：ASTRO BOT · 星舰完整界面与 DualSense 桌面宠物。
- WorkBuddy：Switch 2 · Adventure All-Stars 1.2.1 首页、会话页和透明配件。
- TRAE Work / TRAE SOLO：Xbox Series X|S · Power Lab 首页、会话页和高对比输入框。
- 5 个可安装 Skill、事件驱动持久化守卫、中英文启动指南和 7 张真实验收截图。

启动提醒：正常打开应用后请等待主题恢复，不要连续点击。WorkBuddy/TRAE 在缺少调试端口时可能进行一次受控恢复重启；制作主题过程中不会主动重启应用。

## English

The first public release includes:

- Codex: the complete ASTRO BOT · Starship UI and DualSense desktop pet.
- WorkBuddy: Switch 2 · Adventure All-Stars 1.2.1 for home and conversation pages, with transparent accessories.
- TRAE Work / TRAE SOLO: Xbox Series X|S · Power Lab for home and conversation pages, with a high-contrast editor.
- Five installable Skills, an event-driven persistence guard, bilingual startup guides, and seven real acceptance screenshots.

Startup note: open the app normally and wait for theme recovery instead of clicking repeatedly. WorkBuddy/TRAE may perform one controlled recovery restart when their debugging endpoint is absent; theme production itself does not restart apps.
